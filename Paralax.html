WASHING

WASH - two bucket method

Two bucket method is one bucket with shampoo wash solution, and one bucket with plain water for rinsing wash mitt. Work on the vehicle from top to bottom, working in small sections, rinsing the wash mitt in rinse bucket out before reloading with shampoo solution from the wash bucket.

Drying

Dry

Dry the car using blower. If a blower is not available dry the vehicle using big and thick microfiber towels.

Claying

Clay

Use good, clean and new clay bar for claying the car. We use plenty of clay bar lubricant.

Prep

Prep

Remove residue by wiping each part which have clayed, Wash again with post clay strips.

Inspect

Inspect

Perform paint correction or remove any scratches if any. See any imperfections by using Led detailor lamp.

Protect

Protect

After paint correction, protect paint by layering carnauba wax or using paint sealant.

Interior

Interior

Using good quality interior trim protectant for cleaning interiors.
